Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

Landing Page of the Day is a curated daily showcase of outstanding landing pages, owned and operated by Landing Page Labs, based in Melbourne, Australia. We highlight great landing page design to inspire marketers and founders.

What data we collect

We collect very little. If you browse the site, we don't collect any personal information from you.

The only time we collect data is if you submit a landing page through the submission form. In that case, we collect:

  • First name
  • Email address
  • Landing page URL
  • Platform information
  • Description of why the page should be featured

This information is used solely to review and potentially feature your landing page.

How we use your data

If you submit a landing page, we use the information you provide to review and feature it on the site. We don't sell, rent, or share your data with anyone for marketing purposes.

Cookies and tracking

We don't use cookies for tracking. We don't run analytics tools on this site. No third-party tracking scripts are loaded when you visit.

Third-party services

We use BunnyCDN (bunny.net) to serve images on the site. BunnyCDN may process standard server logs (like IP addresses) as part of delivering content. You can review their privacy policy at bunny.net/privacy.

We don't use any other third-party services that process visitor data.

Data retention

Featured landing page data is kept for as long as the entry is live on the site. If you want your entry removed, get in touch and we'll take care of it.

Your rights

If you've submitted a landing page and want it updated or removed, just reach out. We're happy to help.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, you can reach us at:
hey@landingpagelabs.co

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle data, we'll update this page. Given the simplicity of this site, we don't expect frequent changes.
